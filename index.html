About Me

Sherlock Holmes is a fictional detective character created by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle, a Scottish writer and doctor. His unique character, charming appearance, as well as methods and ways of thinking that are different from the average person make him highly admired by fans of the character. Even so popular, this character entered the Guinness Books of Records as the most played character, whether in theatre, film, TV series, animation, or radio drama.
